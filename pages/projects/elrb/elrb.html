about this game

welcome to the experiment labs research base, where cutting-edge science meets controlled chaos. what started as a routine research facility has become something far more... interesting.

our team of brilliant (and slightly unhinged) scientists have been pushing the boundaries of what's possible, and the results are... well, let's just say they're not what we expected.

explore the depths of sublevel 5, witness the arcstar reactor in action, and discover what happens when you mix ambition with a healthy dose of scientific curiosity.

experience unique mechanics that blend puzzle-solving with narrative exploration in a mysterious research environment. discover the inner workings of a real research facility, complete with authentic equipment and procedures.
