Legal Information & Notices

1. Intellectual Property Rights

All content created by Rare Sheep Studios, including but not limited to games, artwork, characters, stories, and branding, is protected by copyright and intellectual property laws. This includes:

  • Game content and mechanics
  • Artwork and visual designs
  • Characters and storylines
  • Logos and branding elements
  • Source code and technical implementations

2. Usage Restrictions

Without explicit written permission, you may not:

  • Create derivative works from our games or content
  • Redistribute or sell our content
  • Use our characters or artwork commercially
  • Reverse engineer our games or software
  • Remove or modify copyright notices

3. Fan Content Policy

We love our community's creativity! You may create fan content under these conditions:

  • Content must be clearly marked as unofficial fan work
  • No commercial use without licensing agreement
  • Must respect our brand guidelines
  • Cannot imply official endorsement

4. Streaming & Content Creation

Content creators may:

  • Stream gameplay with commentary
  • Create game reviews and tutorials
  • Monetize videos featuring our games
  • Use game footage in compilations

5. Trademark Usage

Our trademarks include:

  • "Rare Sheep Studios" name and logo
  • Game titles and logos
  • Character names and designs
  • Product packaging and marketing materials

6. Licensing & Commercial Use

For commercial use of our IP:

  • Contact our licensing team
  • Submit detailed proposal
  • Wait for written approval
  • Follow provided guidelines

7. Modding Policy

Game modifications are allowed under these conditions:

  • Mods must be free and non-commercial
  • Cannot contain inappropriate content
  • Must not violate third-party rights
  • Cannot bypass game security

8. Copyright Enforcement

We protect our IP through:

  • Regular monitoring for infringement
  • DMCA takedown notices
  • Legal action when necessary
  • Cooperation with platforms and authorities

9. Reporting Violations

Help protect our IP:

  • Email: ip@raresheepstudios.com
  • Include detailed description and evidence
  • Provide violator's contact information if available

10. Legal Contact

For legal inquiries:

  • Legal Department: legal@raresheepstudios.com
  • Licensing: licensing@raresheepstudios.com
  • Business Development: bizdev@raresheepstudios.com
